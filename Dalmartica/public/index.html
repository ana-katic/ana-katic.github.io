About Me

This Is My Story

Hello!

Hi! I'm Ana, a 27-year-old from Croatia with a soul that's passionate about bringing creativity into every corner of life.

While my educational journey led me through the world of economy accounting and revision, my heart pulled me toward the newfound passion of web development. As I brought my handmade ideas to life, the desire to share them with others sparked the idea of creating my own blog—a place where everything I create and explore could gather in one spot. It's my personal space, easily accessible for me to relive the moments and creations. While I enjoy using platforms like WordPress, I found myself drawn to the broader world of web development. The process is fascinating and ability to build everything exactly as I imagine it adds an extra layer of excitement to my creative journey. Why settle for simplicity when you can add spice to it? :)

Dalmartica is all about sharing my distinct vision of inspiration through art. It is more than an exhibition, it is a documentation of an aesthetic journey. Join me on this adventure. Come along! Uncover something unique for yourself, share it, integrate it into your life, and let it be your inspiration.
